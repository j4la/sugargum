About Sugargum

Sugargum is a four-piece indie rock band hailing from Australia. Formed by Mannix, Tom, Ryan, and Sam, the band draws inspiration from the natural landscapes of their home, crafting music that's as powerful and raw as the Sugargum trees. Their sound is a blend of guitar-driven rock and melodic hooks that keep you coming back for more.
